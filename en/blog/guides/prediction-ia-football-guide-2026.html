🧠 Why AI Beats Experts

Human experts are victims of cognitive biases — tendency to overvalue recent performances, emotional attachments to certain teams, inability to process hundreds of variables simultaneously. AI has no bias.

It processes objectively all available data: advanced statistics, physical data, complete history, odds markets — in seconds. A human analyst would take hours for the same work and would inevitably make mistakes.

⚙️ How IASHARK Works

1. Data Collection

Every morning, our system automatically collects data from API-Football (statistics, formations, injuries), weather APIs, and real-time odds from 5 bookmakers. Over 50,000 data points per match day.

2. Predictive Model

Our model based on Claude from Anthropic analyzes 200+ variables and generates probabilities for each outcome: home win, draw, away win, Over/Under 1.5/2.5/3.5, BTTS, Double Chance, halftime/final, and more.

3. Value Bet Detection

AI probabilities are compared to odds from 5 bookmakers. When the difference exceeds 5%, a value bet is flagged with the exact edge and recommended stake according to the Kelly criterion.

4. Confidence Score

Each prediction receives a score from 1 to 10. Score ≥7 = strong conviction. On matches scored ≥7/10, our success rate exceeds 60% — or +6 points above chance.
