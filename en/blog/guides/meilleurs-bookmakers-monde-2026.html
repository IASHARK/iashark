🌍 Bookmakers by Region

Western Europe

Betclic, Winamax and Unibet dominate France, Portugal and Belgium. Bwin is #1 in Germany and Austria. Bet365 covers all of the UK and Ireland. All are subject to strict licenses guaranteeing fund safety.

Asia & Middle East

1xBet and Bet365 dominate the Asian market. In Korea, Japan and Thailand, bettors mostly use offshore bookmakers accessible via VPN. Cryptos are particularly popular in this region to bypass banking restrictions.

Africa

Betway is the absolute leader in South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya and Ghana. SportPesa is very popular in East Africa. Mobile money betting (M-Pesa, etc.) is the norm. Booming market — less competition, more opportunities.

Latin America

Betano dominates Brazil and Portugal. Betclic is strong in Spanish-speaking South America. 1xBet is rapidly gaining market share. Football passion is maximum — the Latin market is the most dynamic in the world.

North America

DraftKings and FanDuel dominate the USA (market regulated state by state). Bet365 and BetMGM are rapidly progressing. In Canada, sports betting has been legal since 2021. Market booming post-legalization.

🔒 Bookmakers with VPN

Blocked by your country from accessing a bookmaker? A VPN can allow you access. The best VPN-compatible bookmakers : 1xBet, Bet365, William Hill, Betway and all crypto bookmakers. Note that using a VPN to bypass geographic restrictions may violate the site's terms of service.
